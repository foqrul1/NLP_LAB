Biography

Dr. Muhammad Firoz Mridha has over 13 years of teaching experience and has more than 50 research papers on Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine learning, Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Universal Networking Language (UNL). His research ranges from Bangla Language Processing (BLP), Solving Ambiguity of Bangla words, WordNet and supervised and unsupervised learning. He also focus on data extraction from natural language as an information extraction paradigm in an increasingly knowledge-based connected world facilitated by the big data.
